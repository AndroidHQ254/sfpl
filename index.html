SWYFLY-P&L LTD

Swyfly-P&L Ltd is a consultancy company domiciled in Ukunda-Diani Municipality, Kenya. It was founded in 2021 and is one of the consultants in Information & Technology, Project Planning & Management, Financial Accounting & Audit, Tour & Travel, Security Services, and Supply of Agricultural Products
such as cereals, vegetables, and natured based products.
The company also offers customer-tailored solutions to the current
technological, financial and project challenges.
